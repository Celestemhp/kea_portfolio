About me

Well, as you guessed, my name is Celeste and I´m 23 years old. I´m from Copenhagen but I also have roots on the Faroe Islands. I´m a junior UX/UI designer, and currently study at Københavns Erhversakademi on 3rd semester. Before I started my study as a graphic designer, I was studying at home in my sparetime and taught myself some of the Adobe programs and learned about what graphic design is. I´m a very creative soul who always is crafting something. I love to paint, draw, do mini prototypes of architecture buidings, read books, or do my wall-decoration art. Oh and of course kryds&tværs - always a winner!

I love to explore and constantly learn new things
that´s what drives me everyday!
